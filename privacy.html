Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-06

Overview

Betusin Travels respects the privacy of travelers and guides. We minimize data collection and store only what is necessary to manage bookings and support your itinerary.

Information We Handle

  • Visit data: Booking and reservation information needed to manage visits and group bookings.
  • Contact messages: Information submitted via contact forms to respond to inquiries from parents or schools.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies for site functionality and analytics. We do not use tracking cookies targeted at travelers.

Your Choices

  • Contact us to review or delete personal data related to a booking or inquiry.
  • Opt out of marketing communications by following unsubscribe instructions in emails.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy or data handling, please use the contact form on the Home page or reach out to our support team.
