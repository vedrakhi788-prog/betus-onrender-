Designing Immersive Travel Experiences

How visuals, textures, and pacing create memorable learning moments.

Published 2026-01-06

Visual style, tactile materials, and gentle motion help children feel immersed and comfortable.

Color & texture

Use bright accents and tactile surfaces to invite exploration without overwhelming senses.

Simple silhouettes

Clear shapes and bold outlines help young children recognize activity stations quickly.

Subtle motion

Gentle animations can draw attention but should never distract from facilitation.
