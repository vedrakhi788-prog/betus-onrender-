Designing Visit Flow & Engagement

Create rhythms that match children's attention spans and support easy transitions.

Published 2026-01-06

Structure visit flow with short, engaging stations and clear transitions to maintain children’s interest.

Natural breaks

Create clear pause points so families can rest or regroup without losing momentum.

Save progress

Where relevant, keep simple markers of completion so children feel their achievements are remembered.

Return prompts

Friendly reminders and visible milestones encourage further exploration during the visit.
